Shiv Shakti Enterprises

Technology blended with precision is the soul of our business. The same reflects in our name. We have a proven record in serving the Automobile and Electrical industry by supplying them Turned, Machined, Cold Forge Components, Fasteners & Small Sheet Metal parts with the highest grade of workmanship. We are led not only by our vision for growth but also by the prosperity of our customers, so we never lose focus on them.

Other Operations

The Secondary operations are backed up by a comprehensive operation department which includes Drilling – Micro Drilling – Tapping – Centerless Grinding – Surface Grinding – Cylindrical Grinding – Threading – Thread Rolling – Vertical & Horizontal Milling – Slotting – Welding and many more. Doing all operation under own control makes us more competitive to deliver the best quality Products. Heat Treatment, Surface Coatings (Zinc Passivation, Geomat, Dacro, Anodizing and Powder Coating) done by well established approved and dedicated suppliers.
