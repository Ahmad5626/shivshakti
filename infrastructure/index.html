SET UP & MACHINES FOR CNC Process

We can make a range of CNC components from basic through to complex.CNC Sliding Head Turning is one of the many services Precitech offer.Sliding head CNC lathes, also known as Swiss Type lathes were designed for machining small high-precision parts and components.

The technology involves a bar being fed through the machine’s headstock and being gripped by a collet. The bar is supported by a guide bush and this helps manufacturers make parts to the highest accuracy and gives a great surface finish.

Another plus is the machines ability to operate unattended overnight which helps our company reduces the cost-per-part.
